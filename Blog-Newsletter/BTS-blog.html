Behind the Scenes


On this week's Behind the Scenes, we introduce another new person where they get to showcase their different interests on our webpage. If you would like to get a chance to feature in the future, you can fill in the form in the Contact page. The upcoming person is a first year student at Queens University Belfast who's name is Ahmed Abdelrehim, an international student originating from Cairo, Egypt

Early Life



My life journey began when I was born in Cairo on 13th March 2004, the second child in my family. After a few years have passed, my family decided to move to the new and upcoming city at that time: Dubai.



Moving to a totally different country was tough on me as a child, as at the beginning I struggled to make new friends, but eventually became more social and open to connect with new people.

Sports



From my earliest memories in Dubai to my latest, I consider Dubai as my home city. Developing throughout my youth life I expanded my knowledge vastly and learnt to build my character. It all kicked off when my dad would teach me some basics of football at the age of 9, later this resulted into me falling in love with the sport. A year later, my dad realised that I should persue my favorite sport in a more competitive manner, registering me at the Spanish Youth Football Academy in Dubai.


History


Over the next few years, I was learning the basics of Ancient Egyptians and their various mythical gods, since I am originally from Egypt this spectacular ancient civlization that existed throughout history stood out in my eyes. This was due to their ahead of time architecture and their unique designs, even some claims arised that the famous Pyramids of Giza could not be made by human beings at that time and that the Ancient Egyptians might have gained some help from extraterrestrial civilizations.

Travel


There are many touristing areas around the globe, but one specifically that I prefer would be the French Alps. Looking over at the breath-taking views of the mountains all around is simply a once in a lifetime experience. Riding in a cable car through the Alps is a surreal moment that could only be described as perfection.

Interests


In addition, I enjoy gaming and watching tv shows. Usually I play fast-paced multiplayer shooter games, one of my top picks is a game called Rainbow Six Siege. As for tv shows, I love watching drama, action, and thriller shows such as Lucifier and Suits.
